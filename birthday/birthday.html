Happy Birthday
August 20th

The Day We
Crossed Paths

August 20th. Crazy how one random day just decides to rearrange your whole life. We crossed paths and honestly? I didn't think much of it. Had no clue that was the beginning of everything.

The Beginning

Too Shy to
Even Say Hi

Weeks. I spent actual weeks trying to talk to you and just... couldn't. Me. The guy who never shuts up. Around you I forgot how words work. That's embarrassing to admit but it's true lol.

Late Nights

The Calls
and the Chaos

Our calls were like an hour, two max. But they hit different. You'd be talking, your roommates would start fighting in the background and I'd just sit there enjoying the whole chaos. Those calls were never long enough but somehow they were always exactly what I needed.

The Chaos

Fights, Jealousy
& All That

We fought so much lol. And yeah I got jealous. Like even the thought of you talking to some other guy would just... I'd lose it. It was dumb and messy and dramatic. But I wouldn't take any of it back. Not one bit.

The Good Days

The Days I
Keep Replaying

Our 5pm snack runs during lecture, sneaking out while everyone pretended to focus. The hangouts where we'd just talk about nothing for hours. I keep replaying those days in my head. The way you'd laugh, the little things you'd say. I didn't know I was making memories. I just knew I wanted to be around you.

Recently

What I Had
to Do

I told you to not talk to me. To leave. And you did. I'm doing good for now. It is what it is. Hope you're doing good too.

Always

I Miss You

I don't say it enough. Or maybe I say it too much. But I miss you. The calls, the fights, the 5pm chaos, all of it. You'll always have a best friend out here, thinking of you. Happy Birthday.

Your forever owl 🦉 ✦
